UX Designer

UX designers focus on design-engineering human-interface products or devices to be easy to use and understand by the targeted cohort. The concept of user-centred design becomes the paradigm in UX design process. It starts with developing user persona, which is researching the user cohort to gain understandings and identifying exactly their needs and wants from the prospective products.

Such human-interface/interaction is not limited to tangible devices but encompasses various human senses, including psychological and emotional reactions. Hence, UX designing is a multi-disciplinary specialty, and UX designers interact with all team members throughout the development process, ranging from market research to conceptualization of products, to protocol testing, and post-marketing analysis.
