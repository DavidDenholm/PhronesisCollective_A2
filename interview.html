Professional IT Interview

What kind of work is done by the IT professional?

Scott Runs a web design and hosting company with allied hosting brands. Scott also runs his own servers, keeping them in a local data center in Sydney to provide a computing power resource service.

What kinds of people does the IT professional interact with? Are they other IT professionals? Clients? Investors? The general public?

Scott deals with marketers, small business owners, other IT professionals such as Windows support, other server hosting professionals and experienced Linux users. It is noted that he usually works individually instead of interacting with other people.

Where does the IT professional spend most of their time?

Scott spends most of his time in an office and partially in a garage in a home environment. Scott travelled out to the East Coast but quickly found that working from home was more convenient, professionally, and personally.

What aspect of their position is most challenging?

  • Time management throughout all aspects of IT
  • Keeping up to date
  • Balance of learning what to learn for current and future business
  • Take breaks and to keep your physical health up instead of working too much
  • Not spending too much money on equipment
